Our Story

Sierra EcoHeat was established with a clear and impactful mission: to turn agricultural and wood waste into clean, sustainable energy solutions for everyday use. Since our inception, we've focused on utilizing abundant but often overlooked resources like coconut shells and sawdust to create eco-friendly cooking briquettes.

Driven by a passionate team of engineers and environmental advocates, we've developed an innovative process that transforms this waste into high-quality, efficient fuel. Our Process

From waste to energy: How we transform coconut shells into premium cooking briquettes
1

Collection

We source coconut shells from local farmers and processors, creating additional income streams for communities.

2

Processing

The shells are cleaned, crushed, and carbonized using our eco-friendly, low-emission technology.

3

Compression

The carbonized material is mixed with natural binders and compressed into uniform briquettes.

4

Drying

Briquettes are dried using solar energy to reduce moisture content and increase efficiency.

5

Distribution

The finished products are packaged and distributed to retailers, restaurants, and direct consumers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coconut shell briquettes

How are coconut shell briquettes better than regular charcoal?

Coconut shell briquettes offer several advantages over regular charcoal: they burn longer (up to twice as long), produce less smoke and ash, generate more consistent heat, and are made from a renewable resource rather than contributing to deforestation. They also have a higher carbon content, making them more efficient.

How long do your briquettes burn?

Our standard briquettes burn for 4-5 hours, while our premium restaurant-grade briquettes can last 6-7 hours under normal cooking conditions. The exact burn time depends on factors like airflow, cooking temperature, and the specific application.

Are your products suitable for all types of cooking?

Yes, our briquettes are versatile and can be used for various cooking methods including grilling, barbecuing, smoking, and in specialized equipment like tandoors, pizza ovens, and commercial kitchens. We also offer custom solutions for specific cooking needs.

Do you offer wholesale pricing for businesses?

We offer competitive wholesale pricing for restaurants, hotels, retailers, and other businesses. Our volume-based pricing ensures that you get the best value for your investment. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

How do you ensure the sustainability of your supply chain?

We work directly with coconut farmers and processors to source shells that would otherwise be discarded. Our production facilities are strategically located near these sources to minimize transportation emissions. We also use solar energy in our drying process and continuously monitor our environmental impact through regular audits and certifications.
